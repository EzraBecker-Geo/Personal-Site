My Projects

The Lithium Mineral Locality Database (LMLD)

This is a consolidated repository of places around the world where hard rock lithium minerals are found. The database consists of 33 fields including geographic, mineral, paragenetic modes, and mineral age information. This project pulled data from Mindat.org and the Mineral Evolution Database

Network Analysis

Mineral networks are an invaluable visual approach to exploring complex mineralogical systems that focus on relationships of minerals and their features through their connections in networks rather than on isolated attributes.

The Art and Utility of Shaping Rocks

My journey into cutting and shaping rock began while working for a custom home builder. I was tasked with carving an arch into a 6-foot-long, 6-inch thick slab of sandstone to be used as a fireplace accent. The finished fireplace turned out great, and I've been passionate about shaping rocks ever since.
